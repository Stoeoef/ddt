Detailed Description

Represents a debate in BPS-format.

BPS (British Parliamentary Style) is a popular, internationally used format for debating. The debate has four different speaker roles, the first and second government and the first and second opposition. Each role consists of two speakers. The debate is judicated by at least one judicator.

This class allows the representation of understaffed debates with less than two speakers in a speaker role or no judicator.
