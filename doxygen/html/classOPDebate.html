Detailed Description

Represents a debate in OPD-format.

OPD (Offene Parlamentarische Debatte) is a popular German format for debating. The debate has 3 different roles, the government, the opposition and the FFR (Fraktionsfreie Redner). Each role consists of 3 speakers. The debate is judicated by at least one judicator.

This class allows the representation of understaffed debates with less than 3 speakers in a speaker role or no judicator.
